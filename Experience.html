Customer service is an essential part of any business. It is the process of providing assistance and support to customers before, during, and after a purchase. Good customer service involves understanding the needs and expectations of the customer, being knowledgeable about the product or service being offered, and being able to communicate effectively with the customer. It also involves being responsive to customer inquiries and complaints, and taking appropriate action to resolve any issues that arise. Ultimately, good customer service is about building strong relationships with customers, which can lead to increased loyalty, repeat business, and positive word-of-mouth referrals.

A Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA) at Rocky Mountain Care is a healthcare professional who provides basic patient care under the supervision of a registered nurse (RN) or licensed practical nurse (LPN). CNAs work in a variety of healthcare settings, including hospitals, nursing homes, and home health agencies. We are responsible for assisting with activities of daily living, such as dressing and grooming, as well as monitoring patients' vital signs and reporting any changes to the RN or LPN. As a CNAs we taken upon labor and may assist with other tasks as needed. To become a CNA, individuals must complete a state-approved training program and pass a competency exam.
